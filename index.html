My story

From industry to classroom

My journey began in the software industry, where I worked for over two decades gaining deep, hands-on experience in programming, systems, and real-world project development.

I realised that most tuition centres focus only on bright students. The slow learner, the student afraid to ask questions, the one who failed — they are left behind. I made it my mission to be the teacher for those very students.

Today I teach from Class 9 all the way to BE, BTech and MCA — both offline in Hooghly and online across West Bengal and beyond.

How I teach

My approach

1
Assess the student first

I begin by understanding exactly where the student is — their comfort level, syllabus, and fear areas.

2
Build from the ground up

No shortcuts. Concepts are built step by step. I never move forward until the student truly understands.

3
Real industry examples

Every concept is connected to how it's actually used in real software projects — not just theoretical definitions.

4
Patience above everything

I never rush a student or make them feel embarrassed. A safe learning environment is essential.
